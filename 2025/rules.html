Tagging Rules and Process

DO NOT BREAK ANY LAWS

You must be granted permission to enter private property



All decisions will be made according to these rules. Make sure to read them so that you know what to expect. Hard evidence such as a video will be prioritized over word of mouth when making decisions.


Tagging Rules:

A tag is only valid if the tagger physically taps their target with their hand. No objects, tools, or projectiles can be used to tag unless the immunity item rules at the time create an exception.

When you tag your target you will obtain their target.

All tags must be recorded on video to be considered valid.

The video must show:

- The tag occurring.

- The tagged player's face.

- Proof of any rules that might apply (immunity items, cars parked, etc.)

- The video must be sent to the official grad tag instagram account within 6 hours of the tag.

- Certain weeks may be free-for-alls without targets

- Your targets will be sent to you through teams.

- You may only tag people who are your target, unless otherwise specified.

- People should not cover there face in order to hide from getting recorded in a tag.

- Targets can't be switched and can't make tags for other people.

- Tagged players will be eliminated from the game.

- You not may break any laws against your target in order to tag them.


Leaderboard:

The leaderboard is ranked in the following order:

For Active Players:

- Number of tags completed

- Most recent tag

For Eliminated Players:

- Number of tags completed

- Time survived

Here is each category and the respective prize*:
- 1st place: $500
- 2nd place: $100
- 3rd place: $50
- Most Tags: $100
- Various unnamed prize categories: ~$100

*The prizes may change at anytime.

The same person may recieve two different prizes

Truces in order to split prizes will not be allowed. Action may be taken if the prize money is. This is in order to protect the spirit of the game.

The expenses are transparent and will be provided to anyone who wishes to see it.


Safe situations where you CANNOT be tagged:

Until only 20 players remain, or May 12, whichever comes first, you will be safe in class from the minute class begins (inclusive) until the minute class ends (exclusive) on the classroom clock.

(for example, in class b block, on non-flex days, targets are safe from 9:22 (inclusive) to 10:45 (exclusive). this means once the clock hits 10:45, targets are no longer safe in their b block classes)

E-block is safe until the teacher dismisses you.

You are in class if:

- Talking to staff or a teacher after class

- You are inside classrooms during a scheduled class

- You are attending a club meeting. Clubs must be registered

- You are doing classwork outside of class during that class' block (e.g. Film Production, Video Production, Photography, or Band). You may not tag people while doing class work outside of the classroom. This is to prevent people from tagging others while not taking any risk themselves.

These situations are always safe:

- Religious spaces and gatherings

- Youth organizations (youth groups, air cadets, etc.)

- During, 10 minutes before, and 10 minutes after, band & athletic practices, and games

- During, 10 minutes before, and 10 minutes after scheduled work shifts and volunteer shifts

- Unscheduled shifts are only safe during the duration of the shift.

- Performing in school events, music concerts, dance performances, or theatre plays.

- During official School field trips (including concerts, tournaments, and competitions)

- Inside the driver's seat of a car that is not parked.

- While taking a test/or assessment.


Situations where you CAN be tagged:

- Any situation not outlined above

- While switching classes

- Going to water a fountain or the bathroom (Don't be weird)

- Spares/Flex blocks even if the target is doing work


Immunity

- Every day will have an immunity item

- The immunity item will be announced for a range of days (a week, a weekend, etc.).

- The rules for each immunity item will be defined once it is announced. Be sure to check the rules to make sure you are fitting the criteria for each week.

- Any person following the immunity item criteria will be immune from getting tagged

- Purges may have exceptionally difficult items to obtain

- You may not steal anyone's immunity item, or have someone else steal anyone's immunity item.

- Two people may not share immunity items, the owner of the item gets priority on the target. If neither own the item the person who was touching it first will get the item.

- You may not attack people in order to prevent a target from reaching their immunity item or knock an immunity item out of someone's hand.


Special Circumstances

You will not gain a tag from this but will get a new target, so it may be in your interest to wait for them to return instead of ratting them out.

If you are only attending careers/disctrict program please message us as we are trying to fix this.

All decisions will be decided by the Grad Tag team.
